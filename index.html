Smart Agriculture

Real-Time Hyperspectral Imaging with Innovative Anomaly Detection

Project Overview

This solution utilizes advanced hyperspectral imaging technology along with real-time anomaly detection methods to transform agricultural monitoring and management practices. The objective is to improve accuracy in identifying crop health issues, pest infestations, and other anomalies that can affect agricultural productivity.

Usage

  • Field Monitoring: Hyperspectral cameras mounted on drones or satellites scan fields, collecting detailed data on crop health and soil conditions.
  • Real-Time Analysis: Advanced algorithms detect anomalies such as pests or nutrient deficiencies in real-time, with results visualized on dashboards for immediate action.
