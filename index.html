Project Objective

To develop a system for target detection by optimizing anomaly detection in hyperspectral images using AI/ML techniques specifically for agriculture.

Applications

  • Pest Detection: Identifying pest infestations early to prevent crop damage.
  • Crop Health Monitoring: Analyzing plant health to optimize irrigation and fertilization.
  • Weed Detection: Differentiating between crops and weeds for targeted herbicide application.
  • Yield Prediction: Estimating crop yield based on health and growth patterns detected in hyperspectral images.
